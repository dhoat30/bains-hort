Why Invest in a Kiwifruit Orchard?

Global Demand

There are many reasons. First, there is a global market. Our orchards grow Zespri Kiwifruit, the worldwide brand leader. An additional benefit of investing in a Kiwifruit orchard is the singular opportunity to also own Zespri stock.

Zespri is not only a global force in the Kiwifruit world, with sales to more than 54 nations, but it is a year-round worldwide fruit seller, offering stockholders another way to build financial security.

21 Months Promise

Kiwifruit is a land-based asset, with a cash crop that yields a return in as little as 21 months following planting. Orchards appreciate in value as they mature, and global demand is poised to grow exponentially as consumers continue to recognise the health and nutritional benefits of this distinctive fruit.

Role Of Bains Hort Group

Beginning to End

Bains Hort Group is involved from beginning to end. You can be confident that the hands-on expertise we have developed over the past two decades offers ample protection for your investment. With a specialised nursery to provide backup for our orchard management efforts, our team is a proven leader in land selection, orchard planning and design, and actual construction of the growing fields. With classic Bruno rootstock for exceptional hardiness, we use G3 and M33 cultivars to produce sturdy plants and delicious fruit.

Complete End to End Solution

As leaders in the industry, Bains Horticulture is a full-service solution for growers, owners and investors. Because we have the means, the knowledge and the experience to address all phases of Kiwifruit orchard development and operation, we bring exceptional value to your investment. From initial land acquisition to actual harvesting, packing and shipping of the ripe fruit, Bains personnel are hard at work to ensure high monetary return.

We manage the entire process, from setting up orchard structures and vertical shelter to irrigation and frost control, planting shelter trees and providing a canopy for the vines. We also oversee necessary thinning, male pruning and all the specific requirements of successful crop management. We are on-site in the Kiwifruit orchards, and on the job for the benefit of every investor.
